QLD Property Law

Why Queensland Is Different

Unlike other Australian states, Queensland requires a qualified solicitor to conduct conveyancing. Guild's QLD team are all qualified legal practitioners.

The standard QLD contract is the REIQ contract of sale. Buyers enjoy a 5-business-day cooling-off period and may terminate, forfeiting only 0.25% of the purchase price.

Our solicitors manage REIQ contract amendments, transfer duty calculations, and Body Corporate disclosures for units and townhouses.
