Who We Are

Built for the Way People Live Today

Most conveyancing firms were built for the old way of doing things — paperwork, waiting rooms, and phone calls that never got returned. We built Guild for how people actually live today.

Our team of qualified conveyancers and property solicitors operate across Victoria, South Australia and Queensland, handling everything electronically via PEXA and secure digital platforms. Our clients never need to set foot in an office.

What hasn't changed is the standard. Every file is reviewed by an experienced professional. Every fee is quoted upfront. Every client has a dedicated point of contact from engagement to settlement.
